How to play CYBER WAR

Each turn, choose a card to bid against the computer for the randomized money in the middle.

The SHAPE indicates a card's POWER. The shape with more sides wins the turn. There is one exception: CIRCLE BEATS STAR!

The NUMBER OF SHAPES indicates a card's VALUE. The winner of the turn receives the winning card's Value plus the money in the middle. Value breaks ties with Power.

The COLOR indicates a card's AURA. Aura breaks ties with Power and Value, with purple the strongest, followed by red, green, and blue. Playing consecutive cards of the same Aura gives a $4 bonus that increases by $4 with each additional card (resets between rounds).

Good luck!
