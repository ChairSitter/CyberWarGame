How to play CYBER WAR

Each turn, pick a card to bid for the randomized central pot. The Shape with more sides wins the turn, with one exception: CIRCLE BEATS STAR! Ties in Shape are decided by the higher Number, and further ties are decided by the most valuable Color: purple (best), red, green, and blue.

Shape, Number, and Color each have one special scoring rule:

SHAPE: If the winning card matches the shape in the middle, a bonus is given equal to the number of sides.

NUMBER: The winner of the turn receives a bonus equal to their card's NUMBER.

COLOR: Playing consecutive cards of the same Color gives a $4 bonus that increases by $4 with each additional card (resets every round).
