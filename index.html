How to play CYBER WAR

Each turn, choose a card to bid for the pot of $1-$20. The card with more sides on its shape wins, with one exception: CIRCLE BEATS STAR!

Ties are decided by number of shapes, then color- purple, red, green, then blue.

SHAPE BONUS: +$5 if the winning card matches the shape shown in the middle.

NUMBER BONUS: Every turn, the winning card gives a $ bonus equal to its number of shapes.

COLOR BONUS: Playing a consecutive card of the same color gives a $4 bonus that increases by $4 with each additional card (resets every round).
