How to play CYBER WAR

Each turn, choose a card to bid for the randomized pot of $1-$30. The card with more sides on its Shape wins, with one exception: CIRCLE BEATS STAR!
Ties are decided by Number of shapes (highest wins), followed by Color- purple, red, green, then blue.

3 scoring bonuses:

SHAPE: If the winning card's Shape matches the shape shown in the middle, a bonus is given equal to that shape's # of sides.

NUMBER: The winner of the turn receives a bonus equal to their card's Number.

COLOR: Playing consecutive cards of the same Color gives a $4 bonus that increases by $4 with each additional card (resets every round).
