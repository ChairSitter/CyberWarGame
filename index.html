How to play CYBER WAR

Each turn, choose a card to bid against the computer for the randomized money in the middle.

The card's shape indicates POWER. The shape with more sides wins the money. There is one exception: CIRCLE BEATS STAR!

The number of shapes indicates a card's VALUE. Whoever wins the turn receives the winning card's value, plus the money in the middle of the board. Value also breaks ties with Power.

The card's color or AURA breaks further ties with Power and Value. Purple is the best, followed by red, green, and blue. Playing the same Aura twice in a row gives a $4 bonus.

Good luck!
